## What is a Fixer?

A **Fixer** is a verified service provider on the Servineo platform who offers professional services to clients (requesters). As a Fixer, you can:

- Create and manage job offers
- Build your professional profile and reputation
- Accept jobs from clients
- Manage your availability through an integrated calendar
- Grow your business through ratings and reviews

## Who Can Become a Fixer?

Fixers on Servineo are skilled professionals in various service categories including:

- **Plomería** (Plumbing)
- **Electricidad** (Electrical)
- **Carpintería** (Carpentry)
- **Pintura** (Painting)
- **Albañilería** (Masonry)
- **Jardinería** (Gardening)
- **Limpieza** (Cleaning)
- And more...

## Key Features

### Profile Management

Your fixer profile includes:

```typescript
interface Fixer {
  id: string;
  name: string;
  email?: string;
  phone: string;
  photo?: string;
  city: string;
  rating?: number;
  completedJobs: number;
  services: string[];
  bio?: string;
  joinDate: string;
  paymentMethods: string[];
  whatsapp?: string;
}
```

### Job Offers

Create detailed job offers with:

- Title and description
- Service category and tags
- Pricing information
- Photos (up to 5 images)
- Location and contact details

### Reputation System

Build your reputation through:

- Star ratings (0-5 scale)
- Client reviews
- Completed job count
- Response time and quality
