## What is Servineo?

Servineo is a comprehensive service marketplace platform that connects people who need services with skilled professionals ready to help. Whether you're looking for home repairs, maintenance, installations, or professional services, Servineo makes it easy to find, hire, and manage service providers in your area.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up as a requester looking for services or as a fixer offering your professional skills
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post or Browse Jobs">
    Requesters post job requests, while fixers browse available opportunities in their area
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect and Schedule">
    Review profiles, communicate through the platform, and schedule appointments
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete and Review">
    Complete the service, process payment, and leave reviews to build trust
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Core Services Supported

Servineo supports a wide range of service categories including:

- **Construction & Repairs**: Masonry, carpentry, painting, waterproofing
- **Home Systems**: Plumbing, electrical, heating, air conditioning
- **Installations**: CCTV, solar panels, home automation
- **Maintenance**: Cleaning, gardening, pool maintenance, fumigation
- **Specialized Services**: Locksmith, mechanics, moving services, and more

<Note>
  Servineo is built with Next.js 15, React 19, and TypeScript, providing a modern, fast, and reliable platform for service marketplace needs.
</Note>
