## Overview

Once you've scheduled an appointment or accepted a job offer, you can track the job's progress through various stages until completion.

## Job Status States

Jobs progress through several states:

### Status Badges

```tsx app/components/fixers/RequestedJob.tsx
function getStateBadgeClasses(state: string) {
  switch (state) {
    case 'accepted':
      return 'bg-emerald-50 text-emerald-700 border-emerald-200';
    case 'pending':
      return 'bg-amber-50 text-amber-700 border-amber-200';
    case 'rejected':
      return 'bg-rose-50 text-rose-700 border-rose-200';
    case 'completed':
      return 'bg-blue-50 text-blue-700 border-blue-200';
    default:
      return 'bg-gray-50 text-gray-700 border-gray-200';
  }
}
```

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pending" icon="clock">
    **Initial State**: Job request submitted, waiting for fixer response
    
    - Badge: Amber/Yellow
    - Fixer is reviewing your request
    - You may receive questions or clarifications
    - No action required from you yet
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Accepted" icon="check-circle">
    **Confirmed**: Fixer has accepted the job
    
    - Badge: Green/Emerald
    - Appointment scheduled
    - Time and date confirmed
    - Prepare for service delivery
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="In Progress" icon="spinner">
    **Active Work**: Fixer is currently performing the service
    
    - Job is being executed
    - Real-time updates available
    - Contact fixer if questions arise
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Completed" icon="circle-check">
    **Finished**: Work has been completed
    
    - Badge: Blue
    - Service delivery finished
    - Ready for review
    - Payment processed
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Rejected" icon="circle-x">
    **Declined**: Fixer could not accept the request
    
    - Badge: Red/Rose
    - May include reason
    - Look for alternative fixers
    - Create new request if needed
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Canceling Jobs

If you need to cancel a scheduled job:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Job Details">
    Navigate to the job from your tracking list
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Cancel Option">
    Click the cancel button in the actions menu
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Provide Reason">
    Optionally explain why you're canceling
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Cancellation">
    Review the cancellation policy and confirm
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Notify Fixer">
    System automatically notifies the fixer
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Cancellation Policy

- **24+ hours notice**: Full refund/no penalty
- **12-24 hours**: Partial cancellation fee may apply
- **< 12 hours**: Cancellation fee applies
- **No show**: Full charge

## Job Completion

When a job is marked complete:

1. **Verify Work** - Check that all agreed work is finished
2. **Take Photos** - Document the completed work
3. **Confirm Completion** - Accept the completion in the app
4. **Process Payment** - Payment is finalized
5. **Leave Review** - Rate your experience
