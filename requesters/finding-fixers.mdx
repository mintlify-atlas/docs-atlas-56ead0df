## Browse Job Offers

The Job Offers page (`/job-offer-list`) is your central hub for discovering fixers and their service offerings.

## Search & Filter Interface

### Search Bar

Use the search bar to find specific services, fixer names, or keywords:

```tsx Components/Job-offers/SearchBar.tsx
<SearchBar onSearch={handleSearchSubmit} />
```

The search system tracks your queries and provides relevant results based on:
- Service descriptions
- Fixer names
- Service categories
- Location data

### Filter Options

Click the **Filter** button to access advanced filtering:

<AccordionGroup>

## Viewing Results

### View Modes

Switch between three different view modes:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="List View">
    Traditional card-based layout showing job offers in a scrollable list with full details.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Map View">
    Interactive map displaying fixer locations with clickable markers for quick browsing.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Grid View">
    Compact grid layout for viewing more offers at once.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

```tsx Components/Job-offers/ViewModeToggle.tsx
<ViewModeToggle
  viewMode={viewMode}
  onChange={handleViewModeChange}
  variant='desktop'
/>
```

### Sorting Options

Sort job offers by:
- **Most Recent** - Newest listings first
- **Oldest First** - Earliest listings first
- **Price: Low to High** - Budget-friendly options first
- **Price: High to Low** - Premium services first
- **Rating** - Highest rated fixers first

```tsx Components/Job-offers/Sort/SortCard.tsx
<SortCard value={sortBy} onSelect={handleSortChange} />
```

## Job Offer Cards

Each job offer card displays:

- **Fixer Name** - Professional service provider name
- **Service Title** - Brief description of the service
- **Category Badge** - Service type (e.g., Plumbing, Electrical)
- **Location** - City or neighborhood
- **Price** - Rate in Bolivianos (Bs)
- **Photos** - Service images or portfolio samples
- **Tags** - Additional service offerings
- **Date Posted** - When the offer was created

<Note>
  Click any job offer card to view the full details in a modal dialog.
</Note>

## Pagination

Browse through results using:
- **Page Numbers** - Jump to specific pages
- **Next/Previous** - Navigate sequentially
- **Results Per Page** - Choose 10, 25, 50, or 100 items per page

```tsx Components/Job-offers/Paginacion.tsx
<Paginacion
  paginaActual={paginaActual}
  registrosPorPagina={registrosPorPagina}
  totalRegistros={total}
  onChange={handlePageChange}
/>
```
