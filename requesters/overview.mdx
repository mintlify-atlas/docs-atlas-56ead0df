## Welcome to Servineo for Requesters

Servineo connects you with verified, skilled fixers in your area who can help with a wide range of services. Whether you need plumbing, electrical work, cleaning, or any other service, Servineo makes it easy to find, hire, and manage service providers.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Fixers">
    Search and filter through hundreds of verified fixers based on location, service type, ratings, and availability.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Profiles">
    View detailed fixer profiles including:
    - Service offerings and specializations
    - Pricing and rate information
    - Customer ratings and reviews
    - Photos of previous work
    - Available promotions and discounts
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Job Requests">
    Submit job requests with:
    - Job description and requirements
    - Preferred time slots
    - Location details
    - Suggested budget
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive Offers">
    Fixers respond to your request with detailed job offers including pricing and availability.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Schedule & Track">
    Accept offers, schedule appointments, and track job progress in real-time.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Rate & Review">
    After job completion, rate your experience and help other requesters make informed decisions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## User Roles

Servineo allows you to switch between requester and fixer roles if you're registered as both. The platform automatically adapts the interface based on your current role.

```tsx Components/calendar/page.tsx
const switchRole = () => {
  setUserRole((prevRole) => (prevRole === 'requester' ? 'fixer' : 'requester'));
};
```
